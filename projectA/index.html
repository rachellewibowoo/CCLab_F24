Scientific Name:

Blobby
the NYU Shanghai water blob

Discovered by/at:

Rachelle on Tuesday, October 15th, 2024 at 12.22 p.m.

Family:

NYU water species

Habitat:

NYU Shanghai Quad center

Appearance:

A circular blob of water

Key Characteristics:

  • Delicate, but gets mad
  • Likes to roam around the quad center to collect water from leaves
  • Gets wierd, angry and bigger when you poke it
  • Randomly occurs when it rains at the NYU Shanghai campus
  • Dies when it gets really angry
  • Without the presence of blobby, the plants around it wither
  • We can ressurect blobby

    • What we know so far about this creature: this creature is delicate but can be very cute yet annoying, it really likes water and the NYU Shanghai Quad and can only live in the NYU Shanghai Quad center (and nowhere else) and only appears when it rains. It dies when it gets really angry and gets angry and angrier when you poke it in a certain period of time. When it dies, the plants around it will wither. It can be ressurected again once we press any key on the keyboard.
